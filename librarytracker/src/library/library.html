Ruskin Bond

The Indian Wordsworth

Born: 1934 India

Known for his evocative descriptions of the Himalayas and simple storytelling style that captures the essence of small-town India.

Fiction Children Memoir

Notable Works:

  • The Room on the Roof
  • Rusty Runs Away
  • Our Trees Still Grow in Dehra

J.K. Rowling

The Wizardry Wordsmith

Born: 1965 UK

Creator of the Harry Potter series which has become the best-selling book series in history and inspired a generation of readers.

Fantasy Fiction Young Adult

Notable Works:

  • Harry Potter Series
  • The Casual Vacancy
  • Cormoran Strike Series

Agatha Christie

The Queen of Crime

1890-1976 UK

Best-selling novelist of all time, known for her detective novels and short story collections featuring iconic characters like Hercule Poirot and Miss Marple.

Mystery Crime Thriller

Notable Works:

  • Murder on the Orient Express
  • Death on the Nile
  • And Then There Were None

Jane Austen

The Pioneer of Romance

1775-1817 UK

Renowned for her witty social commentary and pioneering development of the romantic novel through her timeless classics.

Romance Fiction Classics

Notable Works:

  • Pride and Prejudice
  • Sense and Sensibility
  • Emma

F. Scott Fitzgerald

Voice of the Jazz Age

1896-1940 USA

Iconic American author whose works captured the essence of the Roaring Twenties and the American Dream's complexities.

Fiction Classics Modernism

Notable Works:

  • The Great Gatsby
  • Tender Is the Night
  • The Beautiful and Damned

George Orwell

The Conscience of a Generation

1903-1950 UK

English novelist, essayist, and critic famous for his dystopian novels that critique totalitarianism and social injustice.

Dystopian Political Satire

Notable Works:

  • Nineteen Eighty-Four
  • Animal Farm
  • Homage to Catalonia
