About Cuttlefish Who Loves Diving

Cuttlefish Who Loves Diving (爱潜水的乌贼) is a renowned Chinese web novelist known for his intricate world-building, deep philosophical themes, and innovative approaches to the xianxia and fantasy genres. He is one of the most influential web authors in China.

His works often blend Eastern cultivation concepts with Western fantasy elements, creating unique settings that explore complex themes of identity, morality, and the nature of power. His most famous work, "Lord of the Mysteries," has been widely praised for its creative magic system and Victorian-inspired setting.

Cuttlefish is known for his meticulous research and planning, with each of his novels featuring completely different settings and power systems. This versatility has earned him a dedicated following and critical acclaim within the web novel community.
